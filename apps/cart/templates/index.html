Welcome to Hole in the Wall Paddling Club.

We are a non-profit kayaking organization based in Anacortes, WA run entirely by volunteer members like you. We offer training sessions, day paddles, and extended overnight camping trips all over the Puget Sound including the coast. Be sure to check out the calendar for upcoming events.

Our Mission

To encourage safe kayaking, stress the importance of training in kayaking skills and safety, protect the environment and have fun.
